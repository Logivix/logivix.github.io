Freight Cost Engineering 2026: The Science of Logistics Profitability

In the current global economy, logistics is no longer just an "expense item"—it is a critical lever for profitability. Freight Cost Engineering is the disciplined application of data science and market intelligence to structure supply chains that move more for less.

At LOGIVIX, we have moved beyond simple negotiations. We use advanced engineering frameworks to help multinational leaders identify hidden waste and capture market opportunities in real-time.

The 3 Pillars of Cost Engineering

  • Dynamic Modal Shifting: Automatically switching between air, sea, and land based on real-time fuel and capacity indexes.
  • Consolidation Intelligence: Merging LTL (Less-than-Truckload) shipments into efficient FTL movements using AI.
  • Regulatory Optimization: Navigating duty drawbacks and trade agreements to lower landed costs.
